Refund or return of broken products?

Easy peasy, our support will help you with either return of a broken product or its refund. You can compose a complaint about the defective product and send us an email about it. Another way is calling us, our phone number can be found in the support section of our website.

In case, you would like to send us an email about the impaired product, we recommend stating following information about the product, thus we can identify your order faster and solve the problem as quickly as possible:

  • Order number
  • Product name and model
  • Date of purchase
  • Description of the defect (what exactly is wrong and when it occurs)
  • Photos or videos showing the issue (if possible)
  • Your contact details (name, email, phone number)

Providing complete and accurate information will significantly speed up the resolution process. Once we receive your request, our support team will review it and contact you with the next steps, including return instructions or refund options.

Response time

Our support teams reply within time period 48-96 hours after a report was sent. Typically, the longest response time of our live line is 30-45 minutes, so you won't wait long for help.

Return instructions

In order to return a product you should either write us an email explaining what is the problem with the product, including aforementioned information earlier. For the product to be shipped back you should wait from us a confirmation letter. In that letter we will inform you about return label with address, so that you know where to send the faulty product. Further instructions will be provided in the email.

Refund timeline

After approval of a refund the average expected time of waiting varies between 4 - 6 business days. After what refund is done right after we receive the impaired product.

Warranty / eligibility

Under the condition that the product lameness was noticed within 14 days after the purchase it's possible to return it. Warranty of a product doesn't apply to cases that were not mentioned in the warranty license and/or were caused by reckless misconduct with product's rules of usage.

Shipping responsibility

Shipment fee is supplied by the manufacturer, in case of a defect with the product. Shipment fee for return of a product is payed by the client.

Exchange option

If a replacement can satisfy clients needs it would be recommended by our support team.

Contact alternatives

Our support possibilities include email chatting, live line support or FAQ section.

Tracking

After receiving the confirmation letter and stating issue type you will receive updates about the product location via email.
