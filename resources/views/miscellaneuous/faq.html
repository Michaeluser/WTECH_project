Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a complaint?

If one of our products is not functioning properly, you can file a complaint by following these steps: First, we recommend checking whether your brand has an authorized service center in Slovakia. Complaints handled by an authorized service center are typically resolved more quickly than those handled by a retailer. You can file a complaint at any of our branches or send the defective item to our address.

How to package returned products?

If the defect becomes apparent within 14 days of purchase, you must bring or send the item in its original packaging.

If the claim is made more than 14 days after purchase, it is sufficient to bring or send the product itself.

How do I know when my order has arrived?

As soon as your order is ready, we will notify you via text message or let you know using our official email address.
